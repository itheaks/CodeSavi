Clean Seas Campaign promotes the right to a healthy environment, including plastic-free oceans

The campaign aims to break humanity’s addiction to unnecessary and avoidable plastic, nearly 11 million tonnes of which end up in the ocean annually.
The right to a safe, clean, healthy and sustainable environment is legally recognized in 155 countries. Actions to protect oceans and seas must include a human rights-based approach,The actions should also build upon the obligations and responsibilities of governments and businesses under international environmental law and human rights instruments.
